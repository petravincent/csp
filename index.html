Clients are demanding more technology-driven consulting services, so MDL and McKinsey Practices are partnering to create Client Service Platforms (CSPs) to up our game and rapidly developing tech solutions based on ideas from CSTs in as little as 12 weeks from idea to first release

Our solutions create impact under three different umbrellas

Automate

  • Cut down the time to execute manual activities such as company-, industry diagnostics and benchmarkings on engagements from weeks to minutes
  • Delivers automated tools by combining existing or creating net new - all with a user-friendly interface and real time features that an be used interactively with clients to support more impactful decision making
  • Cut down weeks of effort to minutes

Accelerate

  • Accelerate the timeline to launch a Minimum Viable Product e.g. as part of EDGE or DBOT engagements
  • Provides you with a best practice e2e journey as well as a number of modular pre-built steps which can be implemented with incremental effort
  • Shorten the design and build time by up to 50%

Analyze

  • Enhance recommendations through data by tapping into McKinsey's proprietary models
  • Uncovers new insights to support data driven decision-making e.g. through scenario planning and advanced analytics
  • Drive cost reductions and revenue enhancement e.g. 20% in supply chain cost
